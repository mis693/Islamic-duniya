🕋 Muhammad ﷺ Shayari

Praises upon the most beloved, mercy to mankind.

"Tere siwa kis ke liye uṭhta hai dil ka jazba,
Naam-e-Muhammad ﷺ hi toh hai rooh ka nasha."

"Phir se ek naat ho jaye uss mehboob-e-Khuda ki,
Jinka har zikr ibadat hai, har baat duaa ki."

"Jinki rehmaton se jag ujjala ho gaya,
Wohi hain Muhammad ﷺ, jinka har kalam faza ho gaya."
